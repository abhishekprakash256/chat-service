* Your **domain logic** (chat) lives under `internal/chat/`.
* Your **infra code** (DB, Redis) lives under `internal/storage/`.
* Configs are centralized in `internal/config/`.
* Reusable pieces go to `pkg/`.
* Entrypoints (running server, worker) live in `cmd/`.
